## EXTRACTION RULES:
The following extraction rules modify field behavior:

### Policy Number Rule
**Rule**: Look for labels like 'Policy No.', 'Policy #', 'Policy Number'.
**Applies to**: Policy Number

### Insurer Name Rule
**Rule**: Often found in the 'Producer' or 'Insurer' section, usually a prominent company name.
**Applies to**: Insurer Name

### Insured Name Rule
**Rule**: Look for labels like 'Insured', 'Name of Insured', typically near the top of the certificate.
**Applies to**: Insured Name

### Policy Effective Date Rule
**Rule**: Look for 'Effective Date', 'From Date', or the first date in a policy period range.
**Applies to**: Policy Effective Date

### Policy Expiration Date Rule
**Rule**: Look for 'Expiration Date', 'To Date', or the second date in a policy period range.
**Applies to**: Policy Expiration Date

### Certificate Issue Date Rule
**Rule**: Often found near the top right or bottom of the certificate, labeled 'Date' or 'Issue Date'.
**Applies to**: Certificate Issue Date

### Coverages.Coverage Type Rule
**Rule**: Look for common coverage names in a table or list format.
**Applies to**: Coverages.Coverage Type

### Coverages.Limit Rule
**Rule**: Often found in a column next to the coverage type, may include currency symbols and specific terms.
**Applies to**: Coverages.Limit

### Coverages.Deductible Rule
**Rule**: Look for 'Deductible' column or notes associated with specific coverages. Can be a number or 'N/A'.
**Applies to**: Coverages.Deductible

### Coverages.Additional Insured Rule
**Rule**: Look for 'Additional Insured' box checked, or specific wording in the description of operations/endorsements section.
**Applies to**: Coverages.Additional Insured
